What is BenefitMitra?

BenefitMitra (meaning "Your Benefits Friend") is a free, independent information platform that helps Indians discover government schemes, subsidies and financial benefits they may qualify for. We are an information and guidance platform — NOT an application service.

We believe every Indian deserves to know about the benefits available to them. Crores of rupees in government benefits go unclaimed every year simply because people don't know they exist. BenefitMitra solves that.

What We Are

  • ✅ An information and guidance platform
  • ✅ A free eligibility checker
  • ✅ A scheme discovery tool
  • ✅ A guide to help you understand and navigate government schemes

What We Are Not
